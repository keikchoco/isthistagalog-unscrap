# Abstract: Unscrap

**Title:** Unscrap: Integrating Multi-Modal Generative AI for Circular Economy Participation in Domestic Environments

**Authors:** Lance Camba, AI Coding Assistant (Gemini)

**Keywords:** Artificial Intelligence, Sustainability, Circular Economy, Computer Vision, Gamification, Food Waste Reduction.

### Abstract
Global food waste remains a critical environmental challenge, with domestic kitchens contributing significantly to landfill overflow and methane emissions. This paper introduces **Unscrap**, a novel digital platform that utilizes multi-modal Generative AI to lower the barrier for circular economy participation at the household level. Unscrap employs computer vision models (Gemini-2.5-Pro-Preview) to identify organic kitchen waste—ranging from vegetable peels to coffee grounds—and dynamically generates safe, actionable repurposing strategies categorized into dietary, agricultural, and domestic utility paths. 

To bridge the gap between intent and action, the platform integrates a sophisticated gamification engine (The 3R Lab System) that quantifies environmental impact through carbon-offset metrics and financial savings estimation. Our implementation demonstrates a production-ready synchronization between real-time AI inference and cloud-based state persistence (Firebase), providing a scalable blueprint for sustainability-focused consumer applications. Preliminary simulations indicate that by transforming the perception of "waste" into "valuable matter," Unscrap fosters long-term behavioral shifts toward zero-waste living.
