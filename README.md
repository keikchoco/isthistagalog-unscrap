# 🍃 Unscrap — Turn kitchen waste into wonder.

Unscrap is an AI-powered smart repurposing platform that transforms
domestic organic waste management into an engaging and interactive
sustainability experience.

Built for Filipino households, Unscrap helps users discover practical
ways to reuse kitchen waste such as vegetable peels, eggshells, coffee
grounds, and food scraps by converting them into useful resources like
compost, fertilizers, natural cleaners, and more.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Unscrap aims to make sustainable living more accessible, rewarding, and
interactive for everyday households.

By combining artificial intelligence, gamification, and community-driven
tools, the platform encourages users to reduce waste while learning
creative and environmentally friendly reuse methods.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🤖 AI Scanner
Take a photo of kitchen scraps and let AI identify the material,
analyze texture and safety, and recommend the best reuse or recycling
methods.

Powered by **Gemini 2.5 Pro Preview**.

### 📦 The Bin
A personal collection system that stores all previously discovered
materials, repurposing methods, and sustainability insights.

### 💬 Scrappy AI
An AI-powered eco companion that provides sustainability advice,
recycling tips, DIY reuse ideas, and answers to waste-related questions.

### 🗺️ Scrap-Map
A community-driven map integration that helps users locate composting
sites, eco hubs, recycling centers, and local sustainability resources.
